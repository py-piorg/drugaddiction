Addiction Help Websites

This collection of websites are resources to help those struggling with drug abuse.

Alcohol.org
Provides authoritative resources and information to illuminate the serious toll that alcohol can have on families & public health.
Recovery.org
Supports and provides resources for the recovery lifestyle, including pre-treatment, treatment, aftercare, and potential relapse.
Rehabs.com
Everything you need to know about rehab and addiction treatment options to meet each individual's unique needs.
ProjectKnow.com
Promoting harm reduction substance education that resonates with parents to support recovery.
DrugAbuse.com
Provides the most complete overview of necessary substance abuse information so that people can know when and how to get help.
Withdrawal.net
Providing answers to frequently asked questions about drug and alcohol withdrawal from industry professionals.
Heroin.net
Illuminates the serious toll that heroin can have on communities and public health by providing authoritative resources to support recovery.
Detox.net
Provides practical "how to" information to guide visitors through finding and financing detox.
VeteranAddiction.org
Helping America’s Veterans access important addiction and mental health treatment services.
Addiction-Treatment.com
The most up-to-date substance abuse treatment information.
Co-Occurring.net
Mental health disorders come in all shapes and sizes. At American Addiction Centers (AAC), we integrate mental health care and addiction treatment for comprehensive care that is unique to each patient.
TreatmentSolutions.com
We offer the solutions for every stage in the Alcohol and Drug Addiction process i.e., withdrawal, detox, aftercare and more.
DrugTreatment.com
Learn more about the different treatment options available to you based on the drug of choice.
AddictionBlog
We deliver medically reviewed latest news, information and resources on drug abuse, addiction, treatment options and recovery.
Treatment4Addiction.com
Showcases the best suited treatment options for the individuals who are struggling with addiction.
PsychGuides.com
Get the Information for the identification of symptoms and signs of psychological disorders, effective treatment and recovery.
AlcoholRehab.com
Addiction treatment resource providing information on substance abuse and showcasing high-quality treatment centers.
